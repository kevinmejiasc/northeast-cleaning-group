Straight answers

Is the online price the real price?

Yes — the estimate covers standard-condition homes at the size you select. If your home needs extra attention (first-time deep clean, heavy buildup), we tell you before the visit, never at your door. The price you confirm is the price you pay.

Are your cleaners vetted and insured?

Every cleaner is background-checked and covered by general liability insurance. Commercial clients can request a certificate of insurance any time.

Do I need to be home?

Nope — most clients leave a door code or lockbox note in their booking. You'll get an on-the-way message and a done message every single visit.

What if I'm not happy with a clean?

Send us a photo within 24 hours and we'll come back and re-clean the area free. No forms, no arguing — that's the guarantee.

Do you bring supplies?

Yes, cleaners arrive with professional supplies and equipment. Prefer we use your products (allergies, specific surfaces)? Just note it in your booking.

How does commercial pricing work?

We do a free 15-minute walkthrough of your space, then send a flat monthly price with the exact checklist and schedule in writing — usually the same day as the walkthrough.
